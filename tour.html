Temples Tour

● TOUR OPTIONS:

SHARING TOUR: You will be with other guest go to the tour.

PRIVATE TOUR: You can choose departure time from the hostel. If you're coulple or just want to do the tour private not with other people we highly recommend this option.

* You must know: The price of the tour are not included with the entrance ticket. You can purchase by website (angkorenterprise.gov.kh) or you can purchase at the Angkor Park Pass Ticket Counters (on the way to the temples).
The driver can only pick up and drop off you from one temple to one temple, he will wait you in front of the temples. If you take tour with English speaking guide the guide will be with you in the temple and tell you about history in the temples.

OTHER TEMPLE TOUR

Activity Tour

Floating Village: The tour is departure from the hostel at 2:30 PM. We will go to Kampong Phluk floating village and visit the market before we go on the big boat. The big boat will driving along the lake for you to visit how the people live on the lake. We will stop at floating forest about 20 to 30 minutes if you are interest about floating forest you can pay for the small boat (wooden boat) is 5.50USD for drive in the floating forest or you can just stay on the big boat and continue to see the sunset at the big lake. After we finish the sunset we will come back to the hostel.

Kulen Mountain Waterfall: Departure from the hostel at 8:30 AM. The guide and driver will pick you up at the hostel, take you to Kulen mountain waterfall and go to see the waterfall, Pagoda and one thousand lingas. Kulen mountain is 60 km away from the hostel.

Note: Please check with the receptionist at least one or two days in advance. Because we need to have 5 people minimum to do this trip.

Cycling Tour: Departure from the hostel at 3:30 PM, House guide will pick you up at the hostel and we will ride a bicycle to having dinner at the Lotus flower farm(6 km away from the hostel) on the way go to Lotus flower farm we will stop at the pagoda (Wat Ahtvea) 30 minutes to tell you about some culture and Cambodia believe. After that, we continued to see the sunset with a small meal at the Lotus flower farm.

Street Food Tour: Departure from the hostel at 5:30 PM. The guide will be pick up you at the hostel and take you to market street 60 near the ticket office (6 km away from the hostel) and we are going to tast 7 foods: Fried Insect, Khmer noodle soup, Banh Chao, Papaya Salad, Num Krok, Tropical fruit and Dessert.

Khmer Cooking Class: There’s no better route into any culture than cuisine. Discover Khmer food with a local in their home on this intimate, small-group cooking class. After pickup by tuk-tuk, shop for ingredients at the local market, then prepare and enjoy a 4-course meal including delights such as hot and sour “tom yam” soup and fish in banana leaf. Choose between three different class times, all capped at just six travelers.

Phare Circus: Be mesmerized by the captivating choreography and hypnotic color of a Phare performance in Siem Reap. Feast your senses on a spectacle that blends riveting theater with folk music and distinctly Cambodian stories. Commend the determination of the young circus artists, many of who are from challenging social and economic backgrounds, as they hypnotize the audience with their energy and emotion. Gain insight into the charity Phare Ponleu Selpak and discover the work the organization does to help vulnerable children and adults in Cambodian society.

Note: You need to take a Tuk Tuk to the Phare Circus at least 30 minutes before the show. The Tuk Tuk will costs 3USD to get there.
